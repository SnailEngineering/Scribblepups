Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

Scribblepups is a drawing app made by Snail Engineering. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

The short version

Scribblepups does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything you do in the app stays entirely on your device.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect any personal information.
  • We do not use analytics or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or tracking technologies.
  • The app contains no code that communicates with any server or external service.

Photo library access

Scribblepups may request access to your photo library for two purposes:

  • Import: You can choose a photo from your library to use as a drawing background. The photo is loaded locally on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.
  • Save: You can save your finished drawing back to your photo library. Saved images remain on your device and are not uploaded or shared by the app.

Photo library access is only used when you explicitly trigger these actions. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.

Sharing

Scribblepups lets you share your drawings using the iOS share sheet. When you choose to share, your device's standard sharing interface opens and you decide where the image goes. Scribblepups itself does not send the image anywhere — the destination is entirely your choice.

Children's privacy

Scribblepups is suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no personal data, it is safe for children to use. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version here with a revised "Last updated" date. Because we collect no data, changes will most likely only reflect new features we add or clarifications to existing language.
